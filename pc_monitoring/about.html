About Us

Our objective was to develop a straightforward and efficient tool for monitoring the real-time status of computer systems, identifying which are online and which are offline. This project underscores our dedication to harnessing technology to address practical challenges, enhance laboratory management, and strengthen our university community. We aim to provide a platform that enables students, faculty, and staff to maintain connectivity and maximize productivity. Thank you for utilizing our solution!
